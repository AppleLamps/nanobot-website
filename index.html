Latest Updates

Feb 16, 2026

FBI Releases Suspect Description

"Male, 5'9"-5'10", medium build, black Ozark Trail backpack"

FBI releases detailed description of suspect: male, approximately 5 feet 9 inches to 5 feet 10 inches tall, with a medium build, carrying a 24-liter black Ozark Trail Hiker Pack. Over 13,000 tips received since case began.

Feb 15, 2026

Video Canvass Expanded to 2-Mile Radius

Authorities request neighbors within 2-mile radius submit all surveillance footage from January 1 through February 2. DNA profile from black glove being entered into FBI's CODIS database after quality control confirmation.

Feb 14, 2026

Person of Interest Released; DNA Glove Match Confirmed

Carlos Palazuelos, a delivery driver detained during traffic stop, questioned and released. Sheriff Nanos confirms "no one has been eliminated." FBI confirms black glove found 2 miles from home matches gloves worn by suspect in surveillance video.

Feb 14, 2026

FBI Reward Doubled to $100,000

The FBI has increased the reward from $50,000 to $100,000 for information leading to Nancy's recovery and/or arrest of those responsible. Over 13,000 tips received since Feb 1.
